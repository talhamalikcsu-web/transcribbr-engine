Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about billing and features.

How does the free video to text converter work?
When you sign in with your Google account, you are automatically granted 3 free credits. You can use these to transcribe any link or file to test our accuracy before deciding to upgrade.
What is your Fair Usage Policy?
Our Pro Plan offers unlimited transcriptions for standard personal and professional use. To ensure high speeds and quality for all users, we enforce a fair usage policy that prevents automated bot activity, account sharing, or excessive bulk processing that disrupts server performance.
How do I cancel my Pro subscription?
You can cancel your subscription at any time directly from your account dashboard or by emailing our support team. Your pro access will remain active until the end of your current billing week.
Is my audio and video file data secure?
Yes. We use industry-standard encryption. Your audio and video files are processed by our AI and then immediately discarded from our servers. We do not store or use your content for training purposes.
How can I contact support?
For any billing issues, technical help, or general questions, please reach out to us directly at info@zotal-technologies.com. We typically respond within 24 hours.
